STROKE WARNING SIGNS — CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY (BE FAST)
----------------------------------------------------
B — BALANCE: Sudden loss of balance or coordination
E — EYES: Sudden vision changes or double vision
F — FACE: Sudden face drooping (ask patient to smile — is it uneven?)
A — ARM: Sudden arm weakness (ask patient to raise both arms — does one fall?)
S — SPEECH: Sudden slurred or strange speech
T — TIME: Time to call 911 immediately if ANY of the above occur

Also call 911 or go to ER for:
- Sudden severe headache with no known cause
- Unusual bleeding that won't stop (due to blood thinner)
- Irregular heartbeat with dizziness or chest pain
